HOW TO REDEEM

IMPORTANT!
Before redeeming a code, log in to your account and make sure you have linked your EA Account to your in-game account. Otherwise, you will be unable to redeem the code.

  • Pay attention to the redemption conditions and validity period. A code cannot be redeemed after it expires.
  • Each redemption code can only be used once.
  • After successful code redemption, your redeemed item will be delivered to you via in-game mail. For issues, contact in-game support or visit EA Help.
  • For FAQs and Terms & Conditions, go to https://go.ea.com/FCM-Code-FAQ
